Democratization of AR

Why ShARe AR?

ShARe's goal is to popularize AR, by democratizing the creation of AR experiences. Without technical know-how or special software, anyone can create custom augmented reality models with ShARe. Using intuitive build tools, ShARe hopes to inspire the creativity of its users, much like Tik Tok or YouTube.

Ultimately, ShARe aims to build a ubiquitous AR platform, where app developers can integrate ShARe's proprietary object viewer to view user-created models.
